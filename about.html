About ClicksAndBits

The app studio behind clean, useful, and a little-bit-playful apps for Android & iOS.

Who we are

ClicksAndBits Apps is the software studio of JEH Electronics, a small independent company based in Idaho, USA. We design and build apps for Android and iOS — a mix of free tools and premium products — with a simple guiding idea: software should be genuinely useful, pleasant to use, and honest about how it works.

You may also know our sister brand, Octey Apps, home to our free, offline family games. ClicksAndBits is where our broader lineup lives — including premium apps and apps that connect to the cloud to sync your data across devices.

What we believe

Privacy you can read

No walls of legalese. Each app tells you what it collects, why, and how — in language a human can actually follow.

Craft over clutter

We'd rather do a few things beautifully than pile on features. Fast, focused, and free of dark patterns.

Fair pricing

Free where it makes sense, premium where it earns its keep. Never surprise charges, never selling your data.

Family friendly

Apps meant for families follow the stricter standards Google Play and Apple require for younger audiences.

The name

Every app starts with a click and runs on bits — the tiny pieces of data that make software come alive. The colorful bits scattering from the phone in our logo are a nod to that: little sparks of possibility, launching upward. It's playful on purpose. Serious software doesn't have to feel cold.

Part of the JEH Electronics family. ClicksAndBits Apps and Octey Apps are both operated by JEH Electronics. Questions about any of our apps? Reach out anytime.
