How cloud sync works

When you enable sync, your content is transmitted over an encrypted connection (HTTPS/TLS) and stored securely on our servers so it stays consistent across your devices. We do not sell your data or use your content for advertising. You can disable sync or delete your account at any time — see “Deleting your data,” below.

Deleting your data

You can delete your account and associated data from within the app's settings, or by emailing privacy@clicksandbitsapps.com. See our account deletion policy for details. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data from your device.
