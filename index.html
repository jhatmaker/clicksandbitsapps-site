Privacy & compliance

A privacy policy for every app — not just the fine print

Google Play and the App Store require clear data disclosures, and we take that seriously. Our master privacy policy covers how we operate across the board, and every individual app has its own page spelling out the specific data it collects, the permissions it requests, and how any cloud features work.
